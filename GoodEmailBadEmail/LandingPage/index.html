Good Email Bad Email

Welcome to "Good Email Bad Email," a game designed to raise awareness about the impact of digital pollution caused by emails. Learn to distinguish good practices from bad ones while having fun.

Discover the game and test your knowledge now by clicking on the image!

Shoot down bad emails and avoid colliding with them. But be careful not to shoot the good ones!

Digital pollution: a crucial issue

Every email sent or received, especially those with large attachments or multiple recipients, contributes to CO₂ emissions. Reducing our digital footprint also involves adopting more responsible habits in email management.

Good Emails vs. Bad Emails

Email with large and avoidable attachments: Bad!

Very old email: Bad!

Spam or disguised advertisement: Bad!

Well-structured and clear email, recently sent by a trusted person: Good!
